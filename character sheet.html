Original Character Sheet

November 13th 2025

This is a character sheet of some characters I made. Since the main purpose was just to get their heights and proportions correct, as well as their colors, I kept the coloring very simple only adding shading to places that didn't really matter. I hope to one day turn these characters into an animated series.

This is the speedpaint. As you can see in the beginning I used a website to get everyone's heights down since I don't have a great sense of size. When drawing everyone I started with their siloutte rather than their skeleton so I wouldn't overthink it too much.

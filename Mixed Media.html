what do I even call this

November 22nd 2025

This was a bit of an impulse piece for me. I was feeling a lot of frustration that I had to get out somehow. I thought the idea of adding actual pictures would be cool and minimize the seriousness of it all (that and the words I spelled out in beads). I didn't do my usual cleanup, opting to keep it messy just because I think cleaning it up would defeat its purpose.

I made this moodboard for the piece. I was aiming for a brighter and more lighthearted feeling than what I gathered and I think I achieved that. I was mostly inspired by a particular scene in the Netflix show Bee and Puppycat where we first discover Bee is a robot and instead of blood coming out of her its pastel colored ribbons. I was also inspired by the anime Madoka Magica. I think they utilize mixed media in a very interesting way.
